About us

With over 23 years of experience, we specialize in Sheet Metal Fabrication, Powder Coating, and Transformer Production. Our 8,000 sq. ft. facility is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery, including an 8-tank Phosphating Station, more than 5 Power Press Machines, 3 Powder Coating Booths, 6 Spot Welding Machines, 6 Compressors, 4 Shearing Cutters, and various other equipment. We follow a strict quality control process, consistently exceeding the quality standards set by our corporate clients, and we are committed to supporting suppliers nationwide.
